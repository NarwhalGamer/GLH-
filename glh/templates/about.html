About Greenfield Local Hub

Greenfield Local Hub is a cooperative of local farmers and food producers based in Greenfield. We believe in transparent, traceable food — so you always know exactly where what you eat comes from.

Our platform connects customers directly with the people who grow and produce their food, cutting out the middleman and keeping money in the local community.

All products are freshly sourced, clearly labelled with allergen information, and delivered or available for collection from our single location hub.
